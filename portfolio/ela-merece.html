Ela Merece

Relationship app between couples

A software project that consisted of creating a social network for couples. A mobile application was developed where couples created their accounts and through the application it was possible to record important dates, follow the mood of your partner and have daily suggestions on how to please your partner on a day to day basis. The project aimed to bring couples closer together, taking advantage of the great popularization of social networks and the great growth of web consumption via mobile.
Ela Merece was implemented using PHP technologies, for the backend (API) and native Android, for the creation of the mobile application.
