Setupmyteam

PLATFORM THAT UNITES PEOPLE, WITH DIFFERENT SKILLS, TO DEVELOP A PROJECT

Project that aims to create a platform of skills, so that it is possible to find the right people to put into practice any project that an individual or institution possesses. The Setumyteam was born of the need to bring people who have the same ideal, principles and will to offer their best to the world. We believe that enabling the union of people with the most diverse abilities is the key to improving the world. Therefore, our slogam is: Create your project; Build your team; change the world!
Setupmyteam was implemented using the NodeJs technologies, for the backend and AngularJS, for the application frontend.
